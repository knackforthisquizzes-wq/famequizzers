What this phase is doing

Sustained Fame is the long middle of visibility — where the public still wants the vibe, and you decide how available the image should be.

These quizzes explore your style under ongoing spotlight: brighten, soften, reframe, or keep distance.

The goal isn’t to keep attention. The goal is to see what constant attention would style you into.

Routine: attention becomes background, not a surprise.
Remix: narratives travel faster than corrections.
Signature: what stays consistent when trends change.

About the project

FameQuizzers is organized into phases because fame isn’t a moment — it’s a trajectory. Sustained Fame is Phase 3: the era where attention lingers long enough to become a look. Later phases move into refinement, leverage, and long-term outcomes.
