What this phase is doing

Celebrity Fantasy is the gloss stage: where visibility looks like glow, status, and being effortlessly interesting in public spaces.

These quizzes stay playful while quietly mapping taste, projection, and how you enjoy being seen.

The goal isn’t “be famous.” The goal is discovering the version of you that photographs best.

About the project

FameQuizzers is organized into phases because attention evolves. Celebrity Fantasy is Phase 1: the version of visibility that feels all glow. Later phases explore refinement, endurance, and what lingers once the lights soften.
