What you're actually doing

FameQuizzers is a set of personality-style quizzes that explore how you’d move through attention if you became “known”, the shiny parts, the surreal parts, and the “wait… people recognize my coffee order?” parts.

It starts shallow on purpose. You get to imagine your celebrity vibe, your origin story, your tolerance for recognition. Then the lens sharpens: narrative, spectacle, endurance, influence, and legacy.

No, this won’t make you famous. It will, however, make you more fluent in fame, which is basically the cleanest outcome.

Fast: short quizzes, easy to binge.
Glossy-honest: it treats fame like a system, not just a crown.
Thought experiments: for your inner “notice me” goblin.

About the project

FameQuizzers is organized into six phases, from the shallow end of celebrity fantasy to the deep end of long-term outcomes. Each hub contains quizzes that build on the last, because attention isn’t a moment, it’s a trajectory.
